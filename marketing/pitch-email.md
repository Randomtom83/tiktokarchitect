## Notes for sending

1. **Personalize the bracketed editor name.** Even just a first name lifts open rate.
2. **Send one publication at a time.** Wait 7–10 days before following up. Don't fan out.
3. **Editor lookups**: ArchDaily masthead, Architect Magazine staff page, Dezeen contributor page. Use the editor whose recent work has been on diversity, education, BIM tools, or digital practice. Skip generic `tips@` addresses.
4. **Best send window**: Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am their local time.
5. **Voice check** before sending: read it out loud. If anything sounds like a robot, kill that line.
6. **AnansiBuild beta status**: every variant says "do not run a download link without checking with me." Keep that in. Reporters do publish download links from press kits.
7. **What is not in any of these emails on purpose**: any reference to the legal record, anything about Sophia, anything personal about family. The strategy is positive surface area, not pre-emptive defense.
