About

Architecture should be for everyone.

23+ years in architectural design. Principal at Green Stories LLC. Associate AIA. LEED Green Associate. Co-founder and former VP of the NJ Chapter of NOMA. Former President of the Montclair Branch of the NAACP.

I started making TikToks because the profession needed a different voice. Somebody who looked like the communities we build in. Not every architect has to come from money or look a certain way.

I built NIA, the Black Architecture Archive. I collaborate with BLKBRY Studios, an architectural firm in Newark. I wrote a children's book about Norma Merrick Sklarek, the first Black woman licensed as an architect in New York and California.

How many Black architects are there in the United States?

There are fewer than 3,000 Black architects in the United States, of whom about 2,500 are men, per the National Council of Architectural Registration Boards (NCARB). Tom Reynolds — known online as @TikTokArchitect — is an architectural designer in New Jersey working his way to becoming one of them, and building tools for the people behind him. He runs a TikTok channel, the NIA archive, and 23 free professional tools for the field.

Source: NCARB demographic reporting.

Who is TikTokArchitect?

Tom Reynolds is an architectural designer based in New Jersey with 23+ years of practice. NJIT, Bachelor of Architecture, 2008. He spent close to nine years as a lead designer at John J. Perkins, AIA on Mack-Cali commercial work. He is principal at Green Stories LLC and the founder of NIA: The Black Architecture Archive. He is currently VP of the New Jersey chapter of NOMA, the National Organization of Minority Architects, which he helped co-found. He posts as @TikTokArchitect, where he has published more than 1,000 short-form architecture education videos, and he has built 23 free professional tools for architects, engineers, and contractors.

What is NIA — The Black Architecture Archive?

NIA is an archive documenting Black contributions to architecture and the built world. It collects architect profiles, video interviews, and data on diversity within the architectural profession. It is built by a designer in active practice, not by a historian after the fact. Hosted at nia.greenstoriesllc.com.

What is Architect the Game?

Architect the Game is a free Android game built by Tom Reynolds about architectural career decisions. From your first all-nighter to the ARE (Architect Registration Examination), every choice branches and every decision costs something. Current version: 1.0.0 (7.6 MB).

What is AnansiBuild?

AnansiBuild is a Revit plugin built by Tom Reynolds that exports architectural BIM models to multi-color 3MF files for 3D printing. It supports 26 3D printers and 19 architectural scales, with automatic material separation. Named after the West African folk hero. Currently in private beta.
