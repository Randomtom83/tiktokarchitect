The work

What he's building

NIA: The Black Architecture Archive

nia.greenstoriesllc.com

Architect profiles. Video interviews. Data on diversity in the profession. Built by a designer in active practice, not by a historian after the fact. There are fewer than 3,000 Black architects in the United States. About 2,500 are men. NIA exists so those stories don't disappear.

Architect the Game

Android · v1.0.0 · 7.6 MB

From your first all-nighter to the ARE, every decision branches. Every choice costs something. Sleep is for the licensed.

AnansiBuild

Revit 2026 · Private Beta

Revit-to-3MF exporter for 3D printing architectural models. 26 printers. 19 scales. Auto material separation. Named after the West African folk hero who carried wisdom across the world in a pot.

ShambaWorks

Android · v1.0.0 · 17.7 MB

A flower bed planning app that goes to the nursery with you. Snap your bed, add flowers as you shop, set a budget — get a visual planting map and shopping list before you check out. From the Swahili word shamba, meaning a cultivated garden.

BalyeCleaner

Windows · v1.0.0

Sweep your project folders clean. Scans for outdated Revit backup files (.0001.rvt, .0002.rvt) and AutoCAD backups (.bak), compares dates against the parent file, and deletes the dead weight — while flagging any backup that’s newer than the original so nothing gets lost.
